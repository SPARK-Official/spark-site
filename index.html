About Us

Sharing • Projects • Art • Real-world • Knowledge

Our mission is to inspire young students to approach everyday life with creativity and curiosity through artistic self expression. Today's students are increasingly disconnected and disengaged with one another, causing students to struggle to think critically and utilize their imagination to its fullest extent.

To address this, we teach important life skills such as communication, problem solving, and resilience through hands-on activities while encouraging them to see the world from new perspectives and reignite their spark.

Founded in early 2026 by two high school students, we have already hosted numerous workshops at local elementary schools.

Based in the Fairfax County area, Northern Virginia.
